The Funk of 'Starboy'

"Starboy" is an upbeat and energetic electro-pop hit by the legendary French duo Daft Punk. The song celebrates success, excess, and the life of a superstar. With its catchy melody and futuristic beats, "Starboy" became an instant classic and went on to win a Grammy Award for Best Dance Recording. Get ready to dance and sing along to this electrifying anthem!
